Case 01
Market Entry Strategy for a European Industrial Group
Developed a comprehensive market entry framework for a mid-cap industrial manufacturer expanding into Latin America. Deliverables included competitive landscape analysis, regulatory mapping, and a phased go-to-market roadmap. The client secured board approval within six weeks.

Case 02
Financial Model for a Series B SaaS Company
Built a three-statement financial model with integrated scenario analysis for a B2B SaaS platform preparing its Series B fundraise. The model supported investor conversations across twelve institutional meetings and contributed to a successful round.
