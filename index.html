Case 01
Market Entry Strategy for a European Industrial Group
Developed a comprehensive market entry framework for a mid-cap industrial manufacturer expanding into Latin America. Deliverables included competitive landscape analysis, regulatory mapping, and a phased go-to-market roadmap. The client secured board approval within six weeks.
Go-to-Market Strategy
Case 02
Financial Model for a Series B SaaS Company
Built a three-statement financial model with integrated scenario analysis for a B2B SaaS platform preparing its Series B fundraise. The model supported investor conversations across twelve institutional meetings and contributed to a successful round.
Financial Modelling
Case 03
Business Model Redesign for a Consumer Health Startup
Advised a consumer health venture on restructuring its revenue model from B2C to a hybrid B2B2C approach. Defined unit economics, pricing tiers, and partnership frameworks that improved path-to-profitability projections and unlocked a new funding narrative.
Business Model Definition
Case 04
Commodity Futures Structuring
Acted as technical intermediary in the structuring of global soybean futures contracts between a Latin American group and a North American counterparty, mediating operational and regulatory divergences, managing bank compliance, creating the ISIN for the instrument, and structuring SWIFT messages for settlement.
