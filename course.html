Curriculum overview

From the cost of a unit to the strategy of a firm —
and back again, with rigour.

This site reorganises the lecture material into six self-contained modules. Each module opens with the underlying intuition, layers in the technical formulas, and then puts the ideas to work through interactive calculators and short cases. Read in order, or jump to whichever topic you need next.
